# thevegan
Vegan meal Planner - Choose days, a full week, or weeks A full week of allergy-safe, non-GMO vegan meals — with a shopping list that shows exactly what to buy at the store, a built-in budget tracker, and recipes for every meal. Lunches are automatic (last night's leftovers). Works at Walmart, Kroger, Sprouts, or Aldi. Download once, use forever.
